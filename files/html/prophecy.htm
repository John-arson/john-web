THE PROPHECY

Once every thousand years, when jupitor aligns with mars and the blood moon is in 5 nights, a great fog will envelop the lands and bring terror to the commonfolk. When 5 short children vanish into the fog He will restore his former glory. The Lord of Darkness sits upon his 3 thrones of bone. Shatter the seal with the wisdom of the sages: A sage of light; A sage of darkness; And one of nothingness; With your newfound powers traverse the 5 lands: Of light; Of darkness; and 3 lands of gray. Return with the children, and be blessed. Return alone and He will rule.
